Withdraw Cash from PayPal – 3 Steps

Withdraw cash out of your PayPal balance - cash is available in as little as 10 minutes*.

Step 1: Log in with PayPal Access

This step allows you to securely access your PayPal account for withdrawals from your computer or mobile device.

Step 2: Select your amount and city

Once you enter the amount you want to withdraw (from your available balance) choose a location (US locations only) to pick up your cash. A transaction fee will be deducted and a MoneyGram Reference Number will be provided.

Step 3: Visit MoneyGram to get your cash

Take your MoneyGram Reference Number, complete the “receive” form, and hand it in. Be ready to show your ID – find a MoneyGram location to withdraw cash today!
