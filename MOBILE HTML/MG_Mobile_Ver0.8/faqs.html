What is the cash service with MoneyGram and PayPal?

This new service, offered by MoneyGram and PayPal, gives consumers a fast, convenient, and secure way to easily get funds into their PayPal account, or someone else’s. Also, get cash out of a PayPal account via a MoneyGram location in as little as 10 minutes*.

How does the cash service with MoneyGram and PayPal work?

PayPal account holders can add cash to their own or someone else’s account via selected MoneyGram locations. The cash is added in minutes*.

PayPal account holder with an available balance can also withdraw cash once a transaction has been initiated on this website. (usually available within 10 minutes*).

  • Find locations to add cash

  • Find locations to withdraw cash

What is an “enrolled” PayPal account?

For security reasons, in order to add cash to the PayPal account, the account will need to have been enrolled at paypal.com.

Simply log on to paypal.com. and if you are directed to the enrollment page, provide the account holder’s identification information, and date of birth.

What information will I need to provide to add cash?

First, set up a US PayPal account if you don’t already have one and enroll it to add cash. If you are adding cash to someone else’s account as a gift, be sure their account is enrolled to add cash.

Visit the Add Cash section of the home page for detailed instructions.

What information will I need to provide to withdraw cash?

After you agree to PayPal sharing your account information with MoneyGram, you will then have to provide a valid ID to pick up the cash.

Where can I use this service to withdraw cash?

Funds can be picked up at any MoneyGram location in US.

Currently, you can add or send cash to your own on someone else’s PayPal account in the US. Additional countries will be added soon!

Withdrawing Cash

How do I withdraw money from my PayPal account with MoneyGram?

For complete directions, visit the Withdraw Cash section of the home page.

How do I pick up my money?

1. Find a MoneyGram location to withdraw cash

2. Bring the transaction Reference Number.

3. Remember to bring a valid photo ID and make sure the name on the ID matches the name on the transaction.

4. Complete the “Receive Money” form at the location, hand it in, and show your ID. Then you will be given your cash.

Who can I send money to from my PayPal account?

Currently, you may not use Withdraw Cash to send money to another person. The service is only available to the account holder to withdraw cash from his or her personal or business balance.

How much does it cost to withdraw cash?

See fees.

What information do I need to provide?

First, set up a US PayPal account if you don’t already have one.

Set up a withdrawal transaction on this website. Once you have a MoneyGram reference number, you will need to go to a MoneyGram location to receive the cash. You will need to bring valid photo ID and make sure the name on the ID matches the name on the transaction.

Refunds

Can I request a refund after I have created a MoneyGram transaction?

If you need to contact MoneyGram regarding a refund, please complete the necessary information on the Contact Us page and select “refund” under reason. If a refund is deemed appropriate, your funds will be credited back into your PayPal account within 10 business days.

In the event you accidently sent money to the wrong PayPal account, please contact PayPal immediately.

*Subject to agent operating hours, availability, local laws and regulations.
