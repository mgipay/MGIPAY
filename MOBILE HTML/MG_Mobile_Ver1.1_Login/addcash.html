Add Cash to PayPal – 3 Steps

Now you can move cash into your PayPal account or someone else’s.

Step 1: Enroll the PayPal account

Log into PayPal. If you see the enroll screen, you’ll need to follow the steps to getting your account enrolled.

Once your account, or the receiver’s account, is enrolled, you’re ready.

Step 2: Find your nearest MoneyGram location to add cash

We’re adding more and more locations, so check to see if there is a participating MoneyGram location near you.

  • Take in your cash and fee
  • Complete a short “Send Money” form

Step 3: Your cash will be added to the PayPal balance within minutes*.

To view fees and transaction limits, see Fees.

*Subject to agent operating hours, availability, local laws and regulations.
