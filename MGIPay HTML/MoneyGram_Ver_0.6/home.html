Add Cash to PayPal – in 3 Easy Steps

Now you can enjoy the freedom to move cash into your PayPal account or someone else’s. Make sure the account is ready to be used at MoneyGram – enroll today.

1Enroll the PayPal account

Make sure your account, or your friend’s account, is ready. Log into PayPal and verify that it’s enrolled. If you don’t see the enroll screen, then you’re already set. If you see the enroll screen, you’ll need to follow the steps to getting your account enrolled.

You’ll need to provide:

  • Account holder’s name and contact information
  • Account holder’s identification

Once enrolled, you’re ready to use participating MoneyGram location to add cash to your, or someone else’s, PayPal account. If you’re adding cash to someone else’s account, just ask them to follow these simple steps. Once that account is enrolled, you’ll be ready to add cash using MoneyGram.

2Find your nearest MoneyGram location to add cash

Currently available in selected MoneyGram locations in New York City, San Francisco, and Dallas. We’re adding more and more locations, so check to see if there is a participating MoneyGram location to add cash near you.

  • Take in your cash and fee
  • Complete a short “send” form
  • Hand it in and show your ID

3Your cash will be added to the PayPal balance within minutes

Now you’re free… to shop, pay, or gift away!

The Freedom to Withdraw Cash from Your PayPal Balance

Now you can enjoy the freedom to withdraw cash from your PayPal balance, giving you flexibility with your spending. Plus, your cash is usually available within 10 minutes. Conveniently speedy is what we’re all about!

1Log in with PayPal Access

From this website or a mobile device, set up your cash withdrawal. Start with logging in – this step allows you to securely access your PayPal account for withdrawals from your computer or mobile device.

2Select your withdrawal amount and city

  • Once you’ve entered the amount you want to withdraw from your available balance and choose a location (US locations only) to pick up your cash, a transaction fee will be deducted and a MoneyGram Reference Number provided.

3Visit MoneyGram to get your cash

Take your MoneyGram Reference Number, complete the “receive” form and hand it in. Be ready to show your ID. The service is currently available at about 47,000 convenient MoneyGram locations in the US – find a MoneyGram location to withdraw cash today!
