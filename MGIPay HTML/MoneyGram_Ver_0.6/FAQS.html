What is the cash service with MoneyGram and PayPal?

This new service, offered by MoneyGram and PayPal, gives consumers a fast, convenient, and secure way to easily get funds into their PayPal account, or someone else’s, and out of their own account, via MoneyGram locations - usually within 10 minutes.

How does the cash service with MoneyGram and PayPal work?

PayPal account holders can add cash to their own or someone else’s account via selected MoneyGram locations. Cash is added in minutes, ready to take advantage of online spending.

PayPal account holder with an available balance can also withdraw cash (usually available within 10 minutes).

  • Find locations to add cash

  • Find locations to withdraw cash

What is an “enrolled” PayPal account?

For security reasons, in order to add cash to the PayPal account, the account will need to have been enrolled at paypal.com.

Simply log on to paypal.com and if you are directed to the enrollment page, provide the account holder’s identification information and date of birth.

What information will I need to provide to add cash?

First, set up a US PayPal account if you don’t already have one and enroll it to add cash. If you are adding cash to someone else’s account as a gift, be sure their account is enrolled to add cash.

Visit the Add Cash section of the home page for detailed instructions.

What information will I need to provide to withdraw cash?

After you agree to PayPal sharing your account information with MoneyGram, you will then have to provide a valid ID to pick up the cash.

Where can I use this service to withdraw cash?

Funds can be picked up at any MoneyGram location in US.

  • Find locations to withdraw cash

What countries can I send money to?

Currently, you can add or send cash to your own on someone else’s PayPal account in the US. Additional countries will be added soon!

  • Find locations to withdraw cash

What are my currency options?

This new service is available in the US and all cash, in and out of your PayPal balance, is available in the US dollar.

Withdrawing Cash

How do I withdraw money from my PayPal account with MoneyGram?

For complete directions, visit the Withdraw Cash section of the home page.

How do I pick up my money?

1. Find a MoneyGram location to withdraw cash Find a Location tool.

2. Bring the transaction Reference Number.

3. Remember to bring a valid photo ID and make sure the name on the ID matches the name on the transaction.

4. Complete the “receive” form at the location, hand it in, and show your ID. Then you will be given your cash.

Who can I send money to from my PayPal account?

Currently, this service is available to withdraw money from either your personal or business PayPal accounts.

How much does it cost to withdraw cash?

See fees above.

What information do I need to provide?

First, set up a US PayPal account if you don’t already have one.

You will need to bring valid photo ID and make sure the name on the ID matches the name on the transaction.

When will the money be available?

Money is usually available for pickup within 10 minutes.

Where can I pick up my money?

Currently, this new service is available in the United States at any MoneyGram location to withdraw cash.

What will prevent my transaction from being available for pick up with 10 minutes?

A couple things will help make sure this happens:

1. Be sure the name on your ID matches the name on the transaction

2. Select the correct state where you will be picking up your cash.

What are the valid forms of identification?

  • Driver's license from any US state or territory

  • State ID card from any US state or territory

  • U.S. Military ID card

  • US Passport

  • US Passport Card (valid in US, US territories Canada, Mexico, Bermuda, and the Caribbean) 

  • Alien Resident card (Alien ID card, Green Card)

Security and Online Account

What information do I need to provide to use this service from MoneyGram and PayPal?

If you already have a PayPal account, you’ll need to first verify it’s enrolled to add money. See the add money section above.

Once enrolled, you’ll need to provide a valid photo ID to complete the transaction and please make sure the name on the ID matches the name on the transaction.

What if I forgot my PayPal user name and/or password?

Visit paypal.com and follow the links for forgot password.

Will these transactions earn MoneyGram Rewards® points?

Yes, when you send money into PayPal accounts (either yours or someone else’s), you will accrue Rewards points.

There will be no Rewards points to do a withdraw from your PayPal balance

Refunds

Can I request a refund after I have created a MoneyGram transaction?

Yes, please complete the necessary information on the Contact Us page (select “refund” under reason) and we will ensure your funds are credited back into your PayPal account within 10 business days.
