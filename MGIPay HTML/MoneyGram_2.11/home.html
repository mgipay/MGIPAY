Add Cash to PayPal – in 3 Easy Steps

Now you can enjoy the freedom to move cash into your PayPal account or someone else’s.

1Check the PayPal account is enrolled

Make sure your account, or your friend’s account, is enrolled and ready. Log into PayPal and verify that it’s enrolled. If you don’t see the enroll screen, then you’re already set. If you see the enroll screen, you’ll need to follow the steps to getting your account enrolled.

You’ll need to provide:

  • Account holder’s name and contact information
  • Account holder’s identification

Once enrolled, you’re ready to use participating MoneyGram location to add cash to a PayPal account, whether yours or someone else’s.

2Find your nearest MoneyGram location to add cash

Currently available at select MoneyGram locations in New York City, San Francisco, and Dallas. We’re adding more and more locations, so check to see if there is a participating MoneyGram location to add cash near you.

  • Take in your cash and fee
  • Complete a short “Send” Money form

3Your cash will be added to the PayPal account within minutes*.

Now you’re free… to shop, pay, or gift away!

The Freedom to Withdraw Cash from Your PayPal Account

Now you can enjoy the freedom to withdraw cash in as little as 10 minutes* from your PayPal account, giving you flexibility with your spending.

1Log in with PayPal Access

PayPal Access allows you to securely log in to your PayPal account on this website or a mobile device and withdraw cash with MoneyGram.

2Select state where you want to pick up your cash and how much money you want to receive

Once you’ve entered the amount you want to receive (from your available balance), choose a location (US locations only) to pick up your cash. A transaction fee will be deducted and a MoneyGram Reference Number will be provided.

3Now you can find your nearest MoneyGram agent location to pick up your cash

Take your MoneyGram Reference Number, complete the “Receive” Money form .Be ready to show your valid photo ID. The service is currently available at about 47,000 convenient MoneyGram locations in the US – find a MoneyGram location to withdraw cash today!
