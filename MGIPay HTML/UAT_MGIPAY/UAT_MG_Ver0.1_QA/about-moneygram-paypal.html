About MoneyGram & PayPal

The MoneyGram brand is recognized throughout the world as a leading international money transfer services company. The diverse array of products and services we offer enables consumers and businesses to make payments and send money around the world. From New York to Russia or London to India, and in approximately 198 countries, MoneyGram's money transfer service moves money quickly and easily around the world.

Our payment services also help businesses operate more efficiently and cost effectively. We offer our products and services to consumers and businesses through a worldwide network of agents and financial institutions.

Global Money Transfer

MoneyGram Money Transfer

Our global money transfer service allows consumers to send and receive money worldwide, primarily through a global network of third-party agents and a number of MoneyGram-owned retail locations in the U.S. and Western Europe that use our money transfer systems. In addition to person-to-person (also known as cash-to-cash), customers have alternatives in money transfer delivery channels such as direct-to-account, ATMs and kiosks for deposit and cash receive, cash-to-a mobile phone and cash-to-card in various markets around the world.

MoneyGram’s alliance with PayPal extends this service offering by allowing consumers to add cash to their own or someone else’s US PayPal account from selected locations and to withdraw cash from a US PayPal balance for collection at any of the approximately 47,000 US MoneyGram locations. Cash can be added or withdrawn within minutes*.

MoneyGram Bill Payment Services

Our bill payment services allow consumers to make urgent payments or pay routine bills through our network to certain creditors ("billers"). We maintain relationships with billers in key industries which include the credit card, mortgage, auto finance, telecommunications, corrections, utilities, satellite, prepaid card and collections industries. Bill payment services also enable consumers to load and reload prepaid debit cards.

For more information, visit the About Us section
